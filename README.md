# 📊 Superstore Business Intelligence Dashboard

Excel | Pivot Table | Data Visualization | Business Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Business Intelligence Dashboard built using the Superstore dataset. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of business performance, sales trends, customer behavior, and profitability metrics. The goal is to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights that support data-driven decision making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

### Business Performance Monitoring

Track key business metrics including Sales, Profit, Orders, and Profit Margin.

### Sales Analysis

Identify top-performing products, categories, and geographical regions that contribute the most to revenue.

### Customer Insights

Understand customer segments, purchasing behavior, and customer contribution to overall business performance.

### Profitability Analysis

Evaluate profitability across products and business segments while identifying factors that negatively impact profit.

## 📊 Dashboard Structure

### 1. Dashboard Overview

Provides a high-level summary of business performance.

**Key Metrics**

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Profit Margin

**Analysis**

* Sales by Year
* Sales by Region
* Top Selling Products

---

### 2. Sales Analysis

Focuses on product and regional sales performance.

**Analysis**

* Sales by Category
* Sales by Sub-Category
* Top 10 Products by Sales
* Sales by State

**Business Questions**

* Which category generates the highest sales?
* Which products contribute the most revenue?
* Which states perform best in terms of sales?

---

### 3. Customer Analysis

Provides insights into customer behavior and segmentation.

**Analysis**

* Sales by Customer Segment
* Top Customers by Sales
* Top Customers by Profit
* Customer Distribution

**Business Questions**

* Who are the most valuable customers?
* Which customer segment contributes the most sales?
* How concentrated is the customer base?

---

### 4. Profit Analysis

Examines the profitability of products and business operations.

**Analysis**

* Profit by Category
* Profit by Sub-Category
* Discount Impact on Profit
* Top Profitable Products
* Loss-Making Products

**Business Questions**

* Which products generate the highest profit?
* Which products are causing losses?
* How does discounting affect profitability?
